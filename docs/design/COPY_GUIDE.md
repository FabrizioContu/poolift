# Poolift Copy Guide

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Principles

1. **Neutral & Inclusive**
   - Never assume the user's context (children, adults, work, etc.)
   - Always provide varied examples
   - Use "persona" or "miembro" instead of "niÃ±o" in generic contexts

2. **Friendly but Professional**
   - Warm without being overly casual
   - Clear and direct
   - Helpful, not condescending

3. **Action-Oriented**
   - Lead with benefits
   - Use active voice
   - Clear calls to action

4. **Consistent Terminology**
   - "Grupo" = permanent collective
   - "Regalo directo" = one-off gift
   - "Miembro" = person in a group
   - "Participante" = contributor to a gift
   - "Organizador" = gift creator/coordinator
